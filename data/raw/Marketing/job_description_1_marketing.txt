JOB TITLE: Social Media Marketing Specialist
COMPANY: Creative Digital Agency
LOCATION: Tirana, Albania
TYPE: Full-time | Hybrid (2 days remote)
SUMMARY
Join our vibrant team as a Social Media Marketing Specialist. You'll manage multiple client accounts, create engaging content, and drive social media growth through creative campaigns.
RESPONSIBILITIES

Manage and grow social media presence across Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and TikTok
Create compelling content (posts, stories, reels, videos)
Develop and implement social media strategies aligned with client goals
Monitor social media trends and apply insights to campaigns
Engage with followers and respond to comments/messages
Analyze performance metrics and prepare monthly reports
Collaborate with graphic designers and content creators
Schedule posts using social media management tools

REQUIREMENTS

Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Communications, or related field
1-2 years experience in social media management (internship counts)
Proficiency in Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, TikTok
Strong copywriting and content creation skills
Knowledge of Canva or Adobe Creative Suite
Understanding of social media analytics
Excellent communication skills in English and Albanian
Creative mindset with attention to detail

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS

Experience with Hootsuite or similar scheduling tools
Basic graphic design skills
Knowledge of influencer marketing
Facebook Blueprint or Google Analytics certification
