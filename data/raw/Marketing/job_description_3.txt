JOB TITLE: Content Marketing Specialist
COMPANY: Tech Company Albania
LOCATION: Tirana, Albania
TYPE: Full-time | Remote
SUMMARY
Seeking a creative Content Marketing Specialist to develop engaging content that drives brand awareness and customer engagement. You'll create blog posts, social media content, videos, and marketing materials.
RESPONSIBILITIES

Write blog posts, articles, and website copy optimized for SEO
Create social media content calendars and execute posting schedules
Develop email newsletters and marketing campaigns
Produce video scripts and assist in video content creation
Conduct keyword research for content optimization
Collaborate with design team on visual content
Monitor content performance and adjust strategy accordingly
Research industry trends and competitor content

REQUIREMENTS

Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Journalism, or Communications
Excellent writing and editing skills in English
Portfolio of written content (blog posts, articles, social media)
Understanding of SEO and content optimization
Creativity and storytelling ability
Familiarity with WordPress or content management systems
Knowledge of social media platforms and trends
Self-motivated and able to work independently

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS

Experience with video editing tools
Graphic design skills (Canva, Photoshop)
HubSpot Content Marketing certification
Knowledge of content marketing analytics
