JOB TITLE: Paid Advertising Specialist
COMPANY: Performance Marketing Agency
LOCATION: Tirana, Albania
TYPE: Full-time | Hybrid (3 days on-site)
SUMMARY
We need a results-driven Paid Advertising Specialist to manage PPC campaigns across Google Ads, Facebook Ads, and other platforms. Focus on maximizing ROI for client campaigns.
RESPONSIBILITIES

Plan, create, and manage paid advertising campaigns (Google Ads, Facebook Ads, Instagram Ads)
Conduct keyword research and audience targeting
Design ad copy and creative concepts with design team
Monitor campaign budgets and optimize for best performance
Perform A/B testing on ads, landing pages, and targeting
Analyze campaign data and provide performance reports
Implement conversion tracking and attribution models
Stay current on platform updates and advertising trends

REQUIREMENTS

Bachelor's degree in Marketing or related field
Hands-on experience with Google Ads and Facebook Ads Manager
Strong analytical skills and data-driven mindset
Understanding of PPC metrics (CPC, CTR, ROAS, conversion rate)
Proficiency in Google Analytics
Excellent attention to detail
Ability to manage multiple campaigns simultaneously
Strong communication skills for client reporting

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS

Google Ads certification (Search, Display, Shopping)
Facebook Blueprint certification
Experience with LinkedIn Ads or TikTok Ads
Knowledge of Google Tag Manager and conversion tracking
Experience with landing page optimization
