JOB TITLE: Brand Marketing Assistant
COMPANY: FMCG Company (Fast-Moving Consumer Goods)
LOCATION: Tirana, Albania
TYPE: Full-time | On-site

Join our Brand team as a Marketing Assistant. Support brand campaigns, market research, and promotional activities for our consumer product portfolio.

Manage relationships with external agencies and vendors
Create marketing materials and presentations
Monitor brand performance metrics
Support product launches and seasonal campaigns
Prepare reports on campaign effectiveness

Bachelor's degree in Marketing or Business
Strong organizational and project management skills
Excellent communication and presentation abilities
Proficiency in Microsoft Office (Excel, PowerPoint, Word)
Creative thinking and problem-solving skills
Ability to work in fast-paced environment
Team player with positive attitude
Attention to detail and deadline-oriented

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS

Internship experience in FMCG or retail sector
Understanding of consumer behavior
Event coordination experience
Knowledge of market research methodologies
