About the job
A career within Deals Transaction Services, will provide you the opportunity to help organizations realize the potential of mergers, acquisitions and divestitures and capital markets. In short, we help some of the world’s leading companies originate, create, execute, and realize value from deals. Through data driven insights we help our clients move in the right direction ensuring maximum value for their company.

As an important member of our team, you would play a key role in strengthening client relationships. You will work closely with the leadership of the team and will be consistently challenged to demonstrate creative thinking and individual initiative.

Your Role:

Assist clients throughout the deal cycle from strategy to successful deal closing;
Participate or lead parts of financial due diligence projects and demonstrate sound understanding of financial analysis, financial reporting requirements, including the mechanics of the financial statements, ask challenging questions about the target’s business and show critical thinking; 
Design, develop, interpret and adapt financial models for the purposes of valuation, sensitivity analysis, business planning and feasibility analyses;
Develop understanding of key engagement objectives and the client’s business, regulatory and industry environment; Write reports and discussion papers;
Support the leadership in the development of new business opportunities, including preparation of proposals and pitches, attendance of presentations and identification of sell-on opportunities. 

What we are looking for:

University degree in Business Administration, Economics, Finance, or Accounting;
Ongoing participation in and/or completion of one of the following professional qualifications is an advantage: CFA, ACCA, CPA and CIMA;
4-6 years of work experience in an assurance, advisory and/or a finance related role;
Previous Big 4 experience will be considered as a plus;
Strong numerical and financial analytical skills and general business knowledge;
Strong report writing skills;
Excellent command of English (spoken and written);
Ability to understand complex problems and devise, communicate and implement workable solutions;
Ability to operate in a multitasking environment;
A confident and outgoing personality, strong communication skills; Strong desire to learn. 

What we offer:

Opportunity to join the a leading Deals team;
Interesting Challenging projects in different industries
Access to global best practices and professional know-how in an international setting
Regional network
Company training and opportunities for career growth
Competitive remuneration and employee benefit programme
