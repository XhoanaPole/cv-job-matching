About the job

Junior Financial Analyst
Tirana Finance Partners | Tirana, Albania
Full-time | Hybrid (3 days on-site)

About Us
Tirana Finance Partners is a growing financial 
advisory and accounting firm serving SMEs and 
corporate clients across Albania. We provide 
services in financial reporting, tax advisory, 
and business consulting. Our team of 45 
professionals is expanding, and we are looking 
for motivated fresh graduates to join our 
analytical team.

The Role
We are looking for a Junior Financial Analyst 
to support our senior team in delivering 
accurate financial reports and data-driven 
insights to our clients. This is an excellent 
opportunity for a recent graduate to gain 
hands-on experience in a fast-paced 
professional environment.

What You Will Do
* Assist in preparing monthly and quarterly 
  financial reports for clients
* Support budget preparation and variance 
  analysis processes
* Maintain and update financial models in Excel
* Reconcile accounts and verify financial data 
  accuracy
* Assist in tax declaration preparation and 
  documentation
* Communicate financial findings to senior 
  analysts and clients

What We Are Looking For
* Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, 
  Economics, or related field
* Strong proficiency in Microsoft Excel
* Basic knowledge of accounting principles 
  and financial statements
* Attention to detail and strong analytical 
  skills
* Good communication skills in Albanian 
  and English
* Ability to work in a team environment
* 0-1 years of experience or internship 
  in finance

What We Offer
* Competitive entry-level salary
* Mentorship from senior finance professionals
* Opportunity to work with diverse clients 
  across industries
* Clear career progression pathway
* Friendly and collaborative team environment

We are an equal opportunity employer and 
welcome applications from all qualified 
candidates regardless of background.
