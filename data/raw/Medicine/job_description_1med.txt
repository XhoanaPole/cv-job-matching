JOB TITLE: Internal Medicine Specialist
COMPANY: American Hospital Tirana
LOCATION: Tirana, Albania
TYPE: Full-time | On-site

SUMMARY
American Hospital Tirana, one of Albania's leading 
private healthcare institutions, is seeking an 
experienced Internal Medicine Specialist to join 
its Medical Department. The successful candidate 
will manage a patient caseload independently, 
lead ward rounds, and contribute to the hospital's 
clinical education programme for junior doctors 
and medical interns.

RESPONSIBILITIES
The Internal Medicine Specialist independently 
diagnoses and manages a broad spectrum of acute 
and chronic medical conditions including 
cardiovascular disease, diabetes, respiratory 
disorders, and infectious diseases. The role 
involves leading daily ward rounds and making 
independent clinical decisions on patient 
management plans without requiring senior 
supervision. The specialist conducts and 
interprets diagnostic investigations including 
ECG, echocardiography referrals, and laboratory 
panels, communicating findings clearly to 
patients and families. Additional responsibilities 
include supervising and mentoring junior doctors 
and medical interns during clinical rotations, 
contributing to departmental audit and quality 
improvement initiatives, participating in 
multidisciplinary team meetings with cardiology, 
endocrinology, and surgery departments, and 
maintaining accurate and timely clinical 
documentation in the hospital EHR system. 
The specialist also participates in the on-call 
rota covering emergency medical admissions 
outside regular working hours.

REQUIREMENTS
- Medical degree from an accredited university 
  with full specialisation in Internal Medicine
- Valid Albanian medical licence and registration 
  with the Order of Physicians of Albania
- Minimum 2-4 years of post-specialisation 
  clinical experience in an Internal Medicine 
  department
- Independent competency in diagnosis and 
  management of complex medical conditions
- BLS and ACLS certification — current and valid
- Proven ability to supervise and mentor 
  junior medical staff
- Strong clinical documentation skills and 
  proficiency in EHR systems
- Excellent patient communication and 
  interpersonal skills
- Fluency in Albanian — English proficiency 
  required for academic and research activities

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS
- Fellowship or advanced training in a 
  subspecialty such as cardiology, 
  endocrinology, or nephrology
- Experience contributing to clinical research 
  or academic publications
- Familiarity with international clinical 
  guidelines including ESC, ADA, and WHO 
  treatment protocols
- Experience in a Joint Commission 
  International accredited hospital setting
