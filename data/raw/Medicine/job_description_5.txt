JOB TITLE: Hospital Resident - Cardiology
COMPANY: Heart Center Tirana
LOCATION: Tirana, Albania
TYPE: Full-time | Residency program
SUMMARY
Seeking motivated medical graduates for our Cardiology residency program. Gain hands-on experience in cardiovascular medicine under expert supervision.
RESPONSIBILITIES

Assist in diagnosing cardiovascular diseases
Perform and interpret ECG readings
Monitor cardiac patients in ICU and wards
Participate in cardiac catheterization procedures
Conduct patient rounds with cardiology team
Learn advanced cardiac imaging techniques
Manage post-operative cardiac patients
Present cases during department meetings

REQUIREMENTS

Recently completed medical degree
Strong interest in cardiology
BLS and ACLS certification mandatory
Excellent clinical and analytical skills
Ability to work long hours and handle pressure
Strong teamwork and communication abilities
Commitment to continuous learning

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS

Research experience in cardiovascular medicine
ECG interpretation proficiency
Knowledge of cardiac pharmacology
Exposure to cardiac imaging (Echo, CT, MRI)
