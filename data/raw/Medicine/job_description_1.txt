JOB TITLE: Junior Doctor - Internal Medicine
COMPANY: American Hospital Tirana
LOCATION: Tirana, Albania
TYPE: Full-time | On-site
SUMMARY
We are seeking a dedicated Junior Doctor to join our Internal Medicine department. You will work under the supervision of senior physicians to provide comprehensive patient care.
RESPONSIBILITIES

Conduct patient examinations and medical histories
Assist in diagnosing and treating common medical conditions
Perform routine medical procedures (IV insertion, wound care)
Monitor patient progress and update medical records
Participate in ward rounds with senior doctors
Respond to emergency situations
Collaborate with nursing staff and other healthcare professionals
Maintain accurate documentation in EHR systems

REQUIREMENTS

Medical degree (MD) from accredited university
Valid medical license to practice in Albania
BLS and ACLS certification
Strong knowledge of internal medicine
Excellent patient communication skills
Ability to work in a team environment
Proficiency in English
Attention to detail and strong work ethic

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS

Internship experience in hospital setting
Knowledge of diabetes and cardiovascular disease management
Familiarity with EHR systems
ECG interpretation skills
