JOB TITLE: General Practitioner
COMPANY: Family Health Clinic
LOCATION: Tirana, Albania
TYPE: Full-time | Clinic hours (Mon-Fri, 8am-5pm)
SUMMARY
We need a Junior GP to provide primary healthcare services to patients of all ages. Manage chronic conditions, perform routine examinations, and coordinate specialist referrals.
RESPONSIBILITIES

Perform routine health check-ups and physical examinations
Diagnose and treat common medical conditions
Manage chronic diseases (diabetes, hypertension, asthma)
Prescribe medications and treatment plans
Order and interpret diagnostic tests
Provide health education and preventive care advice
Coordinate referrals to specialists
Maintain accurate patient records using EHR

REQUIREMENTS

Medical degree from accredited institution
Valid medical license
Strong diagnostic and clinical skills
Excellent interpersonal and communication skills
Knowledge of common medical conditions
Proficiency in EHR systems
Time management and organizational skills

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS

Experience in primary care setting
Knowledge of chronic disease management
Familiarity with preventive medicine
Community health experience
