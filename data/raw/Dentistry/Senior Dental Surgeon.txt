
### JOB TITLE

Senior Dental Surgeon

### ORGANIZATION

Alba Dental Hospital

### LOCATION

Tirana, Albania

### EMPLOYMENT TYPE

Full-Time

---

## POSITION OVERVIEW

Alba Dental Hospital is seeking an experienced Senior Dental Surgeon to provide advanced dental care services and oversee complex treatment procedures within our multidisciplinary dental department. The selected candidate will contribute to clinical leadership, patient care excellence, and professional mentorship initiatives across the organization.

The ideal candidate should possess strong expertise in restorative and surgical dentistry, excellent patient communication abilities, and experience managing advanced dental treatment procedures within professional clinical environments.

---

## CORE RESPONSIBILITIES

* Perform complex dental procedures including surgical extractions, restorative treatments, and oral rehabilitation procedures.
* Evaluate patient oral health conditions and develop comprehensive treatment plans.
* Supervise junior dentists and collaborate with dental hygienists and assistants during clinical procedures.
* Ensure compliance with clinical safety, sterilization, and healthcare quality standards.
* Review diagnostic imaging and patient treatment documentation.
* Participate in multidisciplinary consultations involving orthodontic, prosthodontic, and surgical treatment planning.
* Maintain accurate electronic patient records and clinical reports.
* Support healthcare quality initiatives and clinical process improvements.
* Contribute to patient education regarding oral health maintenance and preventive care.

---

## REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS

* Degree in Dentistry and active professional license.
* Minimum 5 years of professional dental experience.
* Expertise in restorative dentistry and oral surgical procedures.
* Strong understanding of dental radiology and treatment planning procedures.
* Excellent leadership and communication skills.
* Ability to manage complex patient cases and multidisciplinary coordination.
* Strong attention to clinical quality and patient safety standards.
* Fluent English communication skills.

---

## ADDITIONAL ADVANTAGES

* Experience with digital dentistry technologies.
* Specialized certification in oral surgery, prosthodontics, or implantology.
* Experience working in hospital-based dental environments.
* Participation in professional dental conferences and continuing education programs.
