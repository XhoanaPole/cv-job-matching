About the job
On behalf of our client, a leading construction company specializing in residential construction projects, building development, and infrastructure projects in Albania, we are looking for a Mechanical Site Engineer to join the technical team in supervising and executing projects on-site in the south of Albania.



Position: Mechanical Site Engineer

Location: South of Albania



The ideal candidate should have experience in construction site supervision, mechanical engineering execution, HVAC systems, plumbing systems, ventilation systems, and mechanical installations for residential and civil construction projects.





Key Responsibilities:

Supervise and monitor mechanical works during the execution phase on-site.
Ensure mechanical installations, HVAC systems, plumbing systems, and ventilation systems are implemented according to technical drawings and construction standards.
Coordinate with construction site teams, subcontractors, project managers, and engineering departments.
Verify the quality of works and materials used in building construction projects.
Monitor HVAC, hydrosanitary, fire protection, and ventilation systems according to project requirements.
Prepare and review construction progress reports, project quantities, and work volumes.
Assist in cost estimation, BOQ verification, and project execution planning.
Provide periodic reporting on construction progress, technical issues, and engineering solutions.
Conduct inspection and testing of mechanical and MEP systems before project handover.
Ensure compliance with construction safety regulations, technical standards, and site procedures.




Qualifications & Requirements:

University degree in Mechanical Engineering.
Previous experience in construction engineering, mechanical site supervision execution.
Experience in residential buildings, civil engineering projects, or commercial construction projects is an advantage.
Good knowledge of AutoCAD, MS Office, and technical documentation.
Ability to read and interpret mechanical drawings, construction plans, and engineering specifications.
Knowledge of construction standards and on-site execution processes.
Strong organizational, communication, and teamwork skills.
Driving license is considered an advantage.




What We Offer:

Competitive salary package.
Professional work environment and career growth opportunities.
Involvement in major residential, infrastructure, and development projects in Albania.
Continuous training and professional development in the construction and engineering sector.
