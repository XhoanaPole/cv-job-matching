### Human Resources Coordinator

**PeopleAxis Albania**
Tirana, Albania
Full-Time

## About the job

PeopleAxis Albania is currently looking for a motivated and organized Human Resources Coordinator to join our growing HR team in Tirana. This opportunity is ideal for recent graduates or candidates with up to one year of experience who are eager to begin a long-term career in human resources and organizational development.

At PeopleAxis Albania, we support local and international companies with recruitment services, employee engagement programs, and workforce management solutions. Our team believes that strong people operations are the foundation of successful organizations. We are committed to creating a collaborative workplace where employees are encouraged to grow professionally and contribute new ideas.

As a Junior HR Coordinator, you will work closely with recruiters, HR specialists, and department managers to support daily human resources activities. You will gain practical exposure to recruitment operations, onboarding processes, employee communication, and HR administration in a professional business environment.

## What You Will Do

* Assist with scheduling interviews and coordinating communication with candidates.
* Review CVs and organize candidate applications within internal recruitment systems.
* Support onboarding activities for newly hired employees by preparing documentation and orientation materials.
* Maintain employee files and update HR databases with accurate information.
* Assist the HR department with preparing reports, presentations, and internal communications.
* Participate in organizing employee engagement activities, workshops, and internal events.
* Help ensure documentation complies with company procedures and labor regulations.
* Support recruitment campaigns through job posting management and candidate follow-up communication.

## What We Are Looking For

* Bachelor’s degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, Psychology, or a related field.
* 0–1 years of experience in HR, administration, internships, or customer-facing roles.
* Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
* Good organizational abilities and attention to detail.
* Familiarity with Microsoft Office applications including Excel, Word, and PowerPoint.
* Professional attitude and willingness to learn.
* Ability to manage multiple responsibilities in a fast-paced office environment.
* Fluent English communication skills, both written and verbal.

## What We Offer

* Supportive and collaborative work environment.
* Professional mentoring from experienced HR professionals.
* Opportunities for career growth and internal advancement.
* Exposure to recruitment and employee management processes across different industries.
* Modern office environment in central Tirana.
* Ongoing training and development opportunities.

PeopleAxis Albania is an equal opportunity employer. We value diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive workplace for all employees regardless of gender, ethnicity, religion, disability, or background.
