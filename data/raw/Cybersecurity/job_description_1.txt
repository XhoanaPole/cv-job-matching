About the job
Information Security Engineer

 

The position: 

We are looking for a hands-on passionate Level Information Security Engineer to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for the administration and configuration of our next generation firewalls, and for the security of our infrastructure.



The main responsibilities of the position include:

Provide operational support to the business
Investigate operational alerts regarding the network infrastructure in a timely manner
Collaborates closely with senior team members and contribute to the projects the team delivers
Reports and stays abreast of current security technologies, threats, and vulnerabilities
Documents and maintains existing documentation related to technologies or procedures used by the team
Help with the administration and maintenance of the organisation’s firewalls
 

Main requirements:

BSc/MSc in Information Technology or other relevant degree
Minimum 3 year working experience in a related position
Hands-on experience with firewall administration will be considered an advantage
Strong interest in security
Demonstrate good knowledge of security technologies such as firewalls, IPS, WAF and VPNs
Basic understanding of various information system technologies (Windows/Linux systems, Active Directory, VMware, databases etc)
Willingness to learn and grow within the company
Analytical and methodical
Critical thinker and analytical
Excellent verbal and non-verbal communication skills. Ability to explain technical concepts to a non-technical audience
Adaptable and fast learner
Good organisational and time management skills
Team player
Entry level network engineering qualifications (CCNA, JNCIA, CompTIA Network +) will be considered an advantage
Ability to work well under pressure and deliver high-quality results within tight deadlines

Food allowance
Intellectually stimulating work environment
Continuous personal development and international training opportunities
