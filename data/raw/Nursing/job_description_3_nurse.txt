### Clinical Care Nurse

NovaMed Health Network
Tirana, Albania
Full-Time

Healthcare is stressful enough. We want nurses who can bring professionalism, calm communication, and practical problem-solving into patient care every day.

NovaMed Health Network is looking for a Clinical Care Nurse with hands-on clinical experience who wants more responsibility, direct collaboration with healthcare leadership, and the opportunity to improve patient care systems inside a modern healthcare organization.

You will work closely with doctors, healthcare coordinators, and operational teams across multiple clinical departments. This is not a role where you simply follow routines. We want nurses who notice problems, communicate clearly, and help improve healthcare experiences for patients and staff.

## What you will do

* Provide direct nursing care and monitor patient conditions during treatment and recovery procedures.
* Administer medications and treatments according to physician instructions and clinical protocols.
* Support patient assessments and healthcare documentation processes.
* Coordinate with physicians and healthcare teams regarding treatment plans and patient progress.
* Assist with emergency response situations and urgent clinical procedures when required.
* Educate patients and family members regarding recovery instructions and medication usage.
* Maintain compliance with infection prevention and healthcare safety standards.
* Help improve workflow coordination and patient communication procedures within the clinic.
* Participate in healthcare quality improvement initiatives and staff collaboration activities.

## What we are looking for

* 2–4 years of professional nursing experience.
* Strong clinical judgment and patient care abilities.
* Excellent communication and teamwork skills.
* Ability to work effectively within fast-paced healthcare environments.
* Familiarity with healthcare documentation systems and patient records management.
* Professional and compassionate approach toward patient care.
* Ability to manage clinical responsibilities with accuracy and attention to detail.
* Fluent English communication skills.

## What we offer

* Competitive salary and healthcare benefits.
* Flexible scheduling and supportive clinical environment.
* Professional development and medical training support.
* Opportunity to work closely with healthcare leadership teams.
* Modern healthcare facilities and updated medical technologies.
* Career growth opportunities across the healthcare network.

We care about nurses who combine technical competence with empathy and accountability. If you want meaningful work where your contribution matters every day, we would love to hear from you.
