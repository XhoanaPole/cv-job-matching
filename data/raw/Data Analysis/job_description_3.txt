JOB TITLE: Data Analyst - Junior Level
COMPANY: Financial Services Company
LOCATION: Tirana, Albania
TYPE: Full-time | On-site


SUMMARY
We are looking for a detail-oriented Junior Data Analyst to support our financial analytics team in delivering insights that drive business decisions.


RESPONSIBILITIES
- Extract and analyze financial data from multiple sources
- Develop and maintain Excel-based reporting models
- Create visualizations using Power BI
- Perform data quality checks and validation
- Assist in forecasting and budgeting analysis
- Document data processes and methodologies
- Support senior analysts in ad-hoc analysis projects


REQUIREMENTS
- Bachelor's degree in Finance, Economics, Computer Science, or related field
- Advanced Excel skills (pivot tables, VLOOKUP, macros)
- Basic SQL knowledge
- Strong analytical and quantitative skills
- Attention to detail and accuracy
- Ability to work with large datasets
- Good communication skills in Albanian and English
- Team player with willingness to learn


PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS
- Understanding of financial concepts
- Experience with Power BI or similar tools
- Python knowledge is a plus
- Previous internship in finance or data analysis
