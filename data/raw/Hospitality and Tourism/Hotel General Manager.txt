We are looking for a Hotel General Manager to lead our Tirana property and take it to the next level.

Who we are
Boutique Hotels Albania is a collection of three independently owned design hotels in Tirana, Berat, and Sarandë. We opened our first property in 2019 and have grown to 85 rooms across the portfolio. We are not a chain. We do not have layers of corporate process. Every decision that matters gets made here, and we need someone who thrives in that environment and wants to build something genuinely great.

What this role is
You will run the Tirana property — a 42-room boutique hotel in Blloku with a rooftop bar and a food and beverage operation that we want to grow significantly. You will own the P&L, lead a team of 18, and be directly responsible for guest experience, revenue performance, and operational standards across all departments. This is a hands-on leadership role, not a desk job.

What you will own
You will have full operational responsibility for the Tirana property across rooms, food and beverage, and guest services. You will lead and develop a team of 18 including front office, housekeeping, F&B, and maintenance, drive revenue through dynamic pricing, OTA management, and direct booking channel development, and maintain a minimum 4.5 rating across Booking.com, TripAdvisor, and Google. You will manage all supplier relationships, procurement, and cost controls, report monthly P&L performance directly to the owners, and contribute to the expansion strategy for the fourth property planned for Gjirokastër in 2027.

What we need from you
We need five or more years of hotel management experience with at least two years in a General Manager or Deputy GM role, a proven track record of improving RevPAR and guest satisfaction scores, and strong F&B knowledge — our rooftop bar is central to the property's identity and revenue. Excellent English and Albanian are essential, Italian is a strong advantage given our guest mix. Experience with Opera PMS and OTA channel management tools is required. You must be comfortable owning decisions and working directly with the founding owners without corporate layers between you.

What we offer
We offer a competitive senior salary well above Albanian market rate, a performance bonus tied to RevPAR and guest satisfaction targets, an accommodation allowance, and direct partnership with the founding owners with real influence over the group's strategic direction. This is a genuine opportunity to shape one of Albania's most exciting independent hotel collections at a critical moment in its growth.
