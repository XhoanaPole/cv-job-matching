JOB TITLE: Tourism Operations Coordinator
COMPANY: Albania Riviera Tours
LOCATION: Tirana, Albania
TYPE: Full-time | Hybrid

SUMMARY
Albania Riviera Tours is one of Albania's leading inbound tourism operators, specialising in cultural heritage tours, coastal packages, and group travel across the Western Balkans. With a portfolio of over 80 active tour products and partnerships with more than 200 hotels and transport providers, we are seeking an experienced Tourism Operations Coordinator to manage tour logistics, supplier relationships, and client services across our domestic and international product lines.

RESPONSIBILITIES
The Tourism Operations Coordinator manages end-to-end tour operations including itinerary planning, supplier bookings, transport scheduling, and accommodation confirmations for individual and group departures. The role involves maintaining active relationships with hotels, restaurants, local guides, and transport providers across Albania and the Western Balkans region, monitoring tour departures in real time and resolving operational issues directly with guides and clients on the ground. The coordinator prepares detailed tour documentation including rooming lists, vouchers, and driver briefings, negotiates seasonal rates and availability with suppliers, and responds to client and agent enquiries within agreed service level timeframes. Additional responsibilities include contributing to new tour product development through destination research and supplier scouting, maintaining accurate records in the company's tour management system, and producing weekly departure and revenue reports for senior management.

REQUIREMENTS
Candidates must hold a Bachelor's degree in Tourism, Hospitality Management, or Geography and bring two to four years of experience in tour operations, a travel agency, or destination management. Strong knowledge of Albanian tourism destinations, cultural heritage sites, and regional geography is essential, along with excellent organisational skills and the ability to manage multiple concurrent departures during peak season. Fluency in Albanian and English is required — Italian or German is a significant advantage. Strong negotiation and supplier management skills and proficiency in tour management or booking software are expected. Flexibility during peak season with extended working hours is required.

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS
Experience working with international tour operators or OTAs, knowledge of the Western Balkans tourism market, familiarity with GDS platforms such as Amadeus or Galileo, previous experience managing group bookings of 20 or more travellers, and an IATA certification or equivalent travel industry qualification are all advantageous.
