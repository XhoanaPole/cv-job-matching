About the job

Front Desk Agent
Sheraton Tirana Hotel  Tirana, Albania
Full-time  On-site  Rotating shifts

About Us
Sheraton Tirana is one of Albania's premier five-star properties, part of the Marriott International portfolio. Located in the heart of the capital, we host business travellers, diplomatic guests, and leisure visitors from across the world. Our front office team is the face of the hotel and sets the tone for every guest experience. We are expanding our team and looking for a motivated hospitality graduate ready to build a career in a world-class environment.

The Role
This is a front-line guest-facing position. You will be the first and last point of contact for our guests, responsible for creating a warm, professional, and memorable arrival and departure experience. You will work rotating shifts including mornings, evenings, and weekends as part of a structured and supportive front office team.

What You Will Do
You will manage the full guest check-in and check-out cycle using Opera Property Management System, handle room reservations and modifications across direct and OTA channels, respond to guest requests and complaints with professionalism, assist with concierge services including restaurant bookings and transport arrangements, process payments and maintain accurate cash reconciliation at end of shift, liaise with housekeeping and maintenance to ensure rooms are ready on time, and support the duty manager during peak periods and VIP arrivals.

What We Are Looking For
We are looking for candidates with a Bachelor's degree in Hospitality, Tourism, or a related field. Fluency in Albanian and English is essential — Italian is a strong advantage given our international guest profile. You should have a warm and professional demeanor with a genuine passion for guest service, willingness to work rotating shifts, and basic familiarity with hotel PMS systems. Strong communication and problem-solving skills are essential. We welcome applications from recent graduates with 0-1 years of experience or internship in a hotel or tourism environment.

What We Offer
Marriott International offers a competitive entry-level salary, Marriott Bonvoy employee benefits including discounted hotel stays worldwide, structured onboarding and training within the Marriott network, a dynamic multicultural team, and a clear pathway toward supervisory and management roles for high performers.

Marriott International is an equal opportunity employer committed to hiring a diverse workforce and providing an inclusive environment for all employees.
