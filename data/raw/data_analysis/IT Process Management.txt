WHAT WILL YOU DO

You will join the team of IT Process & Program Management and in particular in the management IT Process Management reporting directly to the manager of the factory IT Process Program Management.



You will have the opportunity to be involved in the IT Process Management activities such as:

· Process Mapping: Document current processes using techniques like BPMN and EPC and determine their Standard Operating Procedures.

· KPIs and Metrics: Define and monitor key performance indicators to measure performance, productivity, and quality.

· Reporting: Create detailed reports and dashboards in ServiceNow to present findings and recommendations.

· Data Analysis: Analyze data to identify trends, patterns, and anomalies.

· Gap Analysis: Compare current performance with desired outcomes to identify improvement areas.

· Process Improvement: Identify and design optimized processes for improvement (using methodlogies such as Lean, DMAIC, and so on).

· Compliance and Standards: Ensure processes adhere to our standards and regulatory requirements.
