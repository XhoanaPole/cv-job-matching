JOB TITLE: Junior Data Analyst
COMPANY: Digital Marketing Agency
LOCATION: Tirana, Albania
TYPE: Full-time | Hybrid (3 days on-site, 2 days remote)


SUMMARY
We are seeking a motivated Junior Data Analyst to join our growing analytics team. You will work closely with senior analysts to support data-driven decision making across marketing campaigns.


RESPONSIBILITIES
- Collect, clean, and analyze marketing campaign data
- Create dashboards and reports using Tableau or Power BI
- Perform statistical analysis to identify trends and patterns
- Collaborate with marketing teams to optimize campaign performance
- Maintain and update databases using SQL
- Present findings to stakeholders in clear, visual formats


REQUIREMENTS
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or related field
- Proficiency in SQL and Excel
- Experience with Python or R for data analysis
- Knowledge of data visualization tools (Tableau, Power BI)
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Excellent communication skills in English
- Ability to work in a team environment
- Attention to detail and accuracy


PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS
- Internship or project experience in data analysis
- Familiarity with Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib
- Understanding of statistical methods
- Experience with Git version control
