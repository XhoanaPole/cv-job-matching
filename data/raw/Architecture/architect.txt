 Role Description

 

This is a full-time, on-site Architect role based in Tirana. The Architect will be responsible for designing and overseeing design projects from conception to completion. Day-to-day tasks include creating architectural plans, collaborating with clients and stakeholders, managing project timelines, ensuring compliance with local standards and regulations, and supervising construction processes to ensure design integrity. The role requires a deep understanding of modern architectural practices and a commitment to innovation and efficiency.



 Qualifications

 

Proficiency in architectural design software such as AutoCAD, Revit, SketchUp, or similar tools
Skills in project management, including planning, scheduling, and overseeing construction progress
Knowledge of local building codes, regulations, and sustainable design principles
Creative problem-solving and attention to detail in architectural design
Strong communication and collaboration skills for client engagement and teamwork
Understanding of environmental and sustainability considerations in construction
Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Architecture or a related field
Professional architectural licensure or the progress towards certification
Previous experience in industrial, commercial, or residential architecture projects is a plus
