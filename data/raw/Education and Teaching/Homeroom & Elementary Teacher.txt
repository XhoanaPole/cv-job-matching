About Us
Albanian International School is an international school located in Tirana, the capital city of Albania. We have been in operation for eleven years and are 
fully accredited by the Ministry of Education and Sport and by WASC Western Association of Schools and Colleges. We are looking for dedicated teachers 
who are flexible and ready for the challenge of teaching multiple subjects and committed to helping the school grow by being active in the community.

Website: ais-tirana.org

Openings: Preschool, Kindergarten and Elementary 
Homeroom Teachers

Job Duties
* Serve as homeroom teacher for assigned grade level
* Teach Math, Science, English and/or Social Studies depending on certification
* Follow curriculum standards and develop daily lesson plans subject to review by the Academic Director
* Assist with occasional extracurricular activities outside school hours
* Help build the school's reputation through community outreach and occasional marketing events
* Write short monthly articles for the school newsletter
* Complete all grading and lesson plans in a timely manner
* Support students by providing additional assistance with daily assignments and projects

Minimum Requirements
* BA or BS degree from an accredited university
* Original diploma and Grade Transcript signed by the University's Dean and Rector
* Previous teaching experience and/or valid teaching certification
* Native fluency in English
* Basic IT proficiency
* Native citizen of the US, UK, Australia, New Zealand, or Canada — or education obtained in one of these countries
* Criminal records check from home country dated within the last 6 months

Benefits
* Round trip airfare from country of origin for foreign hires
* Rent-free modern apartment (teacher pays utilities and internet only)
* Work permit and visa tax fees paid by the school
* Residence permit provided
