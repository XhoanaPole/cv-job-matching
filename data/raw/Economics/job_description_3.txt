About the job

Albania Economic Insights Institute is seeking a motivated Economic Research Assistant to support economic analysis, market research, and data interpretation activities within our research and consulting division. This position is ideal for recent graduates interested in macroeconomic analysis, financial research, policy evaluation, and economic reporting.

Our organization works with private companies, public institutions, and international development partners to analyze economic trends, business environments, and sector performance across Albania and the Western Balkans. We value analytical thinking, accuracy, and evidence-based decision-making.

As an Economic Research Assistant, you will support economists and analysts with data collection, report preparation, statistical analysis, and market monitoring activities while gaining exposure to professional economic research methodologies.

What You Will Do
Assist with collecting and organizing economic and financial data.
Support preparation of economic reports, market studies, and analytical presentations.
Conduct research regarding industry trends, inflation, employment, and market performance.
Monitor local and international economic developments.
Assist with statistical analysis and interpretation of economic indicators.
Prepare charts, summaries, and documentation for internal and external reports.
Support project coordination and stakeholder communication activities.
Participate in meetings related to economic policy and market research initiatives.
What We Are Looking For
Bachelor’s degree in Economics, Finance, Statistics, or related field.
0–1 years of professional or internship experience.
Strong analytical and numerical reasoning skills.
Familiarity with Microsoft Excel and basic statistical analysis.
Good research and report writing abilities.
Strong communication and organizational skills.
Fluent English communication skills.
What We Offer
Professional mentorship and training opportunities.
Exposure to real-world economic and market analysis projects.
Supportive and collaborative research environment.
Career development opportunities within the organization.
Modern office environment in Tirana.

Albania Economic Insights Institute is an equal opportunity employer committed to professional growth and analytical excellence.
