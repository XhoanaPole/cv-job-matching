### JOB TITLE

Legal Advisor

### COMPANY

Albania Corporate Partners

### LOCATION

Tirana, Albania

### TYPE

Full-Time

## SUMMARY

Albania Corporate Partners is seeking a qualified Legal Advisor to support legal operations, compliance management, and contract administration activities for domestic and international business clients. The selected candidate will provide legal guidance across corporate, employment, and commercial matters while collaborating with management and external stakeholders.

This role requires a legal professional with practical experience handling contracts, regulatory compliance, legal risk analysis, and client advisory responsibilities. The ideal candidate should demonstrate strong analytical thinking, professional judgment, and the ability to manage multiple legal matters simultaneously.

## RESPONSIBILITIES

* Draft, review, and negotiate commercial contracts, service agreements, and partnership documentation.
* Provide legal guidance regarding labor law, corporate governance, and compliance matters.
* Conduct legal due diligence and support transactional legal processes.
* Monitor changes in Albanian legislation and advise management regarding regulatory implications.
* Collaborate with finance, HR, and operations departments on legal and compliance-related issues.
* Assist with dispute resolution procedures and communication with external legal representatives.
* Prepare legal reports, memoranda, and internal policy documentation.
* Ensure company activities comply with applicable legal and regulatory requirements.
* Participate in meetings with clients, regulatory institutions, and business partners.
* Maintain legal records and documentation according to internal compliance standards.

## REQUIREMENTS

* Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Law.
* Minimum 2–4 years of professional legal experience.
* Strong knowledge of Albanian corporate and commercial law.
* Experience drafting and reviewing legal agreements and documentation.
* Excellent legal research and analytical skills.
* Strong written and verbal communication abilities in English and Albanian.
* Ability to work independently and manage deadlines effectively.
* Professional understanding of confidentiality and legal ethics.

## PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS

* Experience working in corporate legal departments or law firms.
* Familiarity with compliance management and regulatory reporting processes.
* Knowledge of intellectual property or data protection regulations.
* Experience supporting international clients or cross-border transactions.
* Membership in the Albanian Chamber of Advocacy is considered an advantage.
