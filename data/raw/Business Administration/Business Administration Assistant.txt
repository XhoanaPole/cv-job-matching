JOB TITLE

Business Administration Assistant

COMPANY

Vertex Business Solutions

LOCATION

Tirana, Albania

TYPE

Full-Time

ABOUT THE ROLE

Vertex Business Solutions is looking for a motivated and organized Business Administration Assistant to support daily operational activities, internal coordination, and administrative processes within our growing business operations team. This opportunity is ideal for recent graduates in Business Administration or related fields who are interested in developing practical experience in corporate administration, operational support, and business coordination.

The selected candidate will work closely with managers, administrative staff, and different departments to ensure smooth business operations and effective organizational communication. We are searching for someone with strong organizational abilities, attention to detail, and a professional attitude who can adapt to a fast-paced business environment.

WHAT YOU WILL DO
Assist with daily administrative and operational activities across departments.
Prepare reports, spreadsheets, and business-related documentation.
Coordinate internal meetings, schedules, and communication activities.
Maintain organized digital and physical company records.
Support project coordination and operational planning tasks.
Assist managers with preparing presentations and business summaries.
Handle email correspondence and communication with clients or partners.
Support procurement and office supply management activities.
Participate in workflow improvement and organizational support initiatives.
Assist with data entry, filing, and documentation management.
WHAT WE ARE LOOKING FOR
Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Management, Economics, or related field.
0–1 years of professional or internship experience.
Strong organizational and multitasking skills.
Good communication and interpersonal abilities.
Proficiency in Microsoft Office tools including Excel, Word, and PowerPoint.
Ability to work professionally within team environments.
Attention to detail and problem-solving mindset.
Basic understanding of business operations and administrative procedures.
Fluent English communication skills.
NICE TO HAVE
Internship experience within administrative or operational environments.
Familiarity with project coordination or reporting activities.
Experience using Google Workspace or digital collaboration tools.
Additional certifications related to administration or business operations.
WHAT WE OFFER
Supportive and collaborative work environment.
Professional training and mentorship opportunities.
Career growth and development within the company.
Exposure to real business operations and corporate workflows.
Modern office environment located in Tirana.
Opportunity to work with multidisciplinary teams and business professionals.

Vertex Business Solutions is committed to creating an inclusive and professional workplace where employees are encouraged to grow, contribute ideas, and develop their careers.
