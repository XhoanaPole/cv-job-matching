JOB TITLE: Structural Engineer
COMPANY: INTEK Engineering
LOCATION: Tirana, Albania
TYPE: Full-time | On-site

SUMMARY
INTEK Engineering is a leading structural and 
civil engineering consultancy in Albania 
providing design and supervision services 
for residential, commercial, and infrastructure 
projects. We are seeking an experienced 
Structural Engineer to lead design work on 
multiple concurrent projects, produce technical 
documentation, and coordinate with 
architectural and MEP teams.

RESPONSIBILITIES
* Produce structural design calculations 
  for reinforced concrete, steel, and 
  masonry structures
* Develop complete structural drawing 
  sets using AutoCAD and Revit
* Perform structural analysis using 
  SAP2000 or ETABS
* Prepare technical specifications, 
  reports, and tender documentation
* Conduct site visits to verify 
  construction compliance with 
  approved structural designs
* Coordinate with architectural, 
  geotechnical, and MEP consultants 
  throughout the design process
* Review contractor shop drawings 
  and respond to technical queries
* Ensure all designs comply with 
  Albanian building codes and 
  Eurocode standards
* Mentor junior engineers and 
  interns on technical tasks

REQUIREMENTS
* Bachelor's or Master's degree in 
  Civil or Structural Engineering
* 2-4 years of experience in structural 
  design in an engineering consultancy
* Advanced proficiency in AutoCAD 
  and SAP2000 or ETABS
* Strong knowledge of reinforced 
  concrete and steel structure design
* Familiarity with Eurocode standards 
  and Albanian building regulations
* Experience producing complete 
  structural drawing sets
* Excellent analytical and 
  problem-solving skills
* Fluent in Albanian and English

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS
* Master's degree in Structural Engineering
* Experience with seismic design 
  and earthquake engineering
* Knowledge of BIM workflows and 
  Revit Structure
* Registration with the Albanian 
  Chamber of Engineers
* Experience with infrastructure 
  projects such as bridges or 
  retaining structures
