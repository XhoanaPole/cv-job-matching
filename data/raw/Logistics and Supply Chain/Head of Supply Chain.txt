We are looking for a Head of Supply Chain to own our end-to-end operations and build a function that actually works at scale.

Who we are
Agro Export Albania is a fast-growing agri-food export company based in Tirana exporting fresh produce, processed foods, and beverages to markets across the EU, the Gulf, and North America. We moved 120 million ALL worth of product last year and we are targeting 200 million this year. Our supply chain has grown faster than our processes, and we need someone who can own it completely and fix what is broken.

What this role is
You will own the entire supply chain from farm-gate procurement through to export shipment and customer delivery. That means supplier relationships, cold chain logistics, customs compliance, freight procurement, and warehouse operations across Albania and two neighbouring markets. You will build the team, set the standards, and be directly accountable to the CEO for on-time export performance and cost per tonne shipped.

What you will own
- End-to-end supply chain strategy and execution covering procurement, cold chain storage, export logistics, and last-mile delivery coordination across all active markets
- Leading and developing a supply chain team of 6 including procurement officers, logistics coordinators, and warehouse supervisors across two locations
- Managing relationships with 120+ agricultural suppliers across Albania, North Macedonia, and Kosovo, including seasonal contract negotiation and quality compliance oversight
- Owning the freight procurement process including carrier selection, rate negotiation, and contract management across road, sea, and air modes to EU and Gulf destinations
- Ensuring full compliance with EU food safety standards, phytosanitary export certification requirements, and Albanian customs regulations across all product categories
- Implementing a proper demand planning and inventory management system to replace the current spreadsheet-based approach that is no longer fit for our volume
- Driving measurable cost reduction across transportation and warehousing without compromising cold chain integrity or delivery reliability commitments to buyers

What we need from you
- 5+ years of supply chain or logistics experience with at least 2 years in a senior or management role with direct team responsibility
- Demonstrable experience in agri-food, FMCG, or export-oriented supply chains where cold chain and phytosanitary compliance are core requirements
- Deep knowledge of EU export documentation, phytosanitary certification, and Albanian and regional customs procedures
- Strong negotiation skills with carriers, freight forwarders, and customs brokers — this is a hands-on role, not a strategy-only position
- Fluent English is non-negotiable given the volume of international freight partner and EU buyer communication
- Data-driven mindset with advanced Excel skills at minimum and ERP implementation or migration experience preferred

What we offer
- Senior salary significantly above Albanian market rate with a performance bonus tied to export volume growth and cost per tonne targets
- Equity participation for the right candidate after a successful first year with demonstrated impact
- Full operational ownership with direct access to the founding team and no bureaucratic layers between you and the decisions that matter
- A real budget to build your team properly and implement the systems the business needs to scale to the next level
